Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including limited to:

  1. Wikimedia Username
  2. Number of files uploaded on a session
  3. Timestamp of upload.
The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The app use 2 third party services that may collect information used to identify you. They are:

  1. The Google authentication service. The authentication service is essential for the application to perform its functions. However, no information regrading your Google account is stored or retained by this application.
  2. The Wikimedia authentication service. The application OAuth consumer is public on meta.wikimedia.org at [1]. Once authenticated, the application stores and retains your Wikimedia username as part of login/stats purposes. The stats information will be later used to populate a counter on the homepage of the application.
Cookies

This Service does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, the application may use third party code and libraries that use “cookies” to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this Service.
